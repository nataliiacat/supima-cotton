High Quality

It is a cotton with extra long and clean fiber. The fabric obtained from weaving is particularly smooth, compact and shiny.

Comfort

It is characterized by excellent elasticity despite being naturally free of elastane. It softly envelops the body as in a caress for a pleasant sensation on the skin.

Resistance

AMERICAN-GROWN LUXURY

American-Grown Luxury

Supima is the marketing brand for American-grown Pima cotton. Established in 1954, the Supima organization was formed in El Paso, Texas in an effort to brand and promote Supima cotton. The Supima name was derived from a blending of the words “Superior” and “Pima."

Today, Supima focuses on partnering with leading brands across fashion and home markets to ensure that consumers have access to and receive top quality products crafted with Supima’s premium cotton.
